Meet The Team

We are problem solvers. And when we first started our own journey into the world of computer science, we recognized that there was a problem to be solved. A lot of the methods used to get kids interested and learning in the field are antiquated, outdated, or just outright unintuitive. We've experienced the path kids take to get their bearings firsthand - and we're working on ways to make the journey as interesting and engaging as possible. We know what to do - because we've been there. And we'll continue to be there as a guide along the way.
