Overview

The Code66 is a smart light that targets the problems of spending large amounts of time at your desk. Equipped with sensors that can tell if a user is at their desk, the Code66 tracks how long a user spends at their desk, and with the help of the Code66 companion app, can send the user notifications indicating when to take breaks as well as provide a detailed report of desk time. The Code66 will also ensure energy efficiency and convenience by turning on the lamp automatically only when a user is at their desk. The Code66's sleek and modern 3D printed design provides an aesthetically pleasing addition to the desk along with all its functionality.

As an added benefit, the Code66 comes with relay support, allowing the user to further customize their desk experience. Additional lights, fans, speakers, and more can be plugged into the Code66, and can be controlled by data the Code66 collects, such as temperature and time.
